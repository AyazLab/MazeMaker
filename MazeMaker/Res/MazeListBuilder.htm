Function:Create and edit maze lists.

The MazeListBuilder can be found under the "Tools" menu.

Use the combo box and add button to insert a "Maze File" or "Text Display" item into the current list.
The list order can be rearranged using the up, down and remove button on the right hand side of the list.
When an item is clicked, its properties are displayed and can be changed on the property sheet, on the right hand side of the dialog.
