Home > Removing Items

To remove items, simply select the item and press the "Delete" key (not the backspace) or the "Delete" button in the toolbar or "Edit" menu.

Be careful about what objects are selected. There is no undo option. If multiple objects are selected, they will all be deleted at once.

For detailed help with selection, see Selecting objects.
